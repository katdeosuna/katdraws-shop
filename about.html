about the artist

Katherine DeOsuna is a soon-to-be-senior at Cal Poly. As a Graphic Communications major she indugles heavily in all things design. Katherine finds solace through watercolor and embroidery. She wants to share her joy (and maybe make a little dough) with others who appreciate art in the same way.
